Main Tasks

Being that my internship revolved around Research and development, my main tasks were:

● Conduct research on new possible functionalities that could be added to the MX + product range based on user feedback
● Utilize the API technology to create a reporting tool based on the Power BI Restful API
● Testing of the MX Entreprise solution for HR Management and reporting possible bugs to developers

Key Takeaways

Throughout this journey, I've learned about what it really takes to perform at the highest level in a competitive work space. My Takeaways are:

● Always comment and take notes of ideas or implementations that you added or are going to add. This keeps every one on the loop.
● Never wait for someone to do your work. If you need something from someone, you have to go to them directly and avoid time wasting activities
● Take advantage of the expertise around you, most of your collegues have gone through similar paths, take their advice.
