M2M Group is at the heart of our evolving digital society, a worldwide group that has been operating for 29 years to provide processing solutions, securities management and secure electronic services.

Since it's inception in Casablanca, Morocco, the company's main vision was to promote digital transformation through simple transactions either between the bank and a customer or even gouvernment. M2M has produced impactful solutions that have now been adopted amongst 70% of the moroccan banks. It also provides cost efficient solutions for merchants by reducing transactions costs and overhead. Since 2007, M2M has been listed in Casablanca Stock Exchange and is valued at 60 Million Dollars.
