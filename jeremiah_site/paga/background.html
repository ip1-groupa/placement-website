002 : Background & History

Paga was founded in early 2009 by Tayo Oviosu, with a purpose to make it simple for one billion people to access and use money. We are solving two key problems that exist around the world - eliminating the use of cash for transactions and access to financial services.

Founder - Tayo Oviosu

Co-Founder - Jay Alabrabra

Locations
