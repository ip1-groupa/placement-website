003 : Products & Clients

Pagatech Limited officially launched its first product, Paga, a consumer mobile payments service, in September 2011, after recieving its operating license from the Central Bank of Nigeria in the prior month.

In the first phase of its operations, the Paga platform allows clients to transfer money and remit funds electronically, for example, the purchase cellphone time or pay bills. Paga also has physical agents who handle the cash in and cash out functions for clients. In its second phase, Paga will integrate mobile and traditional financial institutions. The firm will deliver branchless financial services to its clients through partnerships with banks, microfinance organizations, and microfinance providers.

Money can be received on any handset in Nigeria with an active phone connection and a valid KYC* registered Paga wallet. You can also receive money directly to any bank account in Nigeria.

Opening a Paga account is very easy and enables you to:

  • Withdraw money at any of our easily accessible Paga agents across Nigeria or by card-less withdrawal at selected ATMs
  • Directly transfer money into any bank account in Nigeria or other Paga wallet
  • Pay bills like Cable TV, internet subscriptions, electricity and other utilities
  • Buy airtime
