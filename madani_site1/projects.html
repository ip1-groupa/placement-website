Consumer Finance

Acquiring

We have been providing POS [Point of Sales] facilities to several merchants in various sectors. In addition, we also provide acquiring facilities for eCommerce merchants who are willing to sell their services/products online.


Leasing

Cim Finance has put together a flying team of dedicated professionals who offer both finance and operating Lease packages to individuals, entrepreneurs, professionals and commercial enterprises for their assets financing.


Factoring

Cim Finance offers domestic Factoring services to a range of commercial clients in Mauritius. We provide solutions to SMEs and big corporates to increase working capital, to effectively manage receivables or simply better manage their cash flow and funding requirements.
