001 : Business Description

Cim Finance is a non-banking financial institution that develops innovative integrated consumer and business products and services, for less privileged communities or for small and medium businesses, which require flexible solutions to improve their quality of life and grow their activities. Cim Finance has the ability to uplift lives and build better futures. A housing Credit Facility can help empower individuals to enhance their families’ comfort build homes for their families; a business Credit Facility can create job opportunities for thousands.

Cim Finance recognises that it has obligations to multiple stakeholders and strive to consistently deliver value to all of them. They believe that dealing with financial services is more than just being a commercially-centred enterprise. Cim Financd is committed to the philosophy of doing the right thing for our customers and seek to positively help assist the communities they are present in, and deliver social value. Their services are created to be low cost, easily accessible, disruptive to traditional practices and amazingly simple to use. Cim Finance's local expertise coupled with their technologically innovative framework, means that they are perfectly placed to develop new products that meet the unique needs of their growing customer base.
