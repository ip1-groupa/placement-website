About me

Who am I?

I’m Madani Napaul, a 23 year-old computer science student at the African Leadership College in Mauritius. I’m from the first batch of the Cim Finance Leadership Scholars & as part of this program, I was offered an internship every summer during my studies at one of their companies in Mauritius & Kenya. Following my internship last summer in Business Process Re-engineering & Digital Transformation, I was offered an internship at Cim Finance again in Digital Product Development & Innovation.

What I enjoy

I love solving complex problems by visualizing end-to-end processes and communicating solutions through sketching. I gained professional experience in product design and innovation strategy, as such, I always look forward to create and involve myself in innovative communities.

Interest

I have a passion for complexities, which drove me to explore data science and machine learning by myself and with the help of the people I engage with. Putting technology & strategy aside, I'm an advanced tennis player who participated in two national level games before a serious knee injury when I was 16. As somebody who lives on an island, chilling at the beach and watching the sunset with some deep house music is something I enjoy by myself and good friends as well.
